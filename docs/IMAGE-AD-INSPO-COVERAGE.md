## Universal output rule

Every observed visual region must end in one of four explicit outcomes:

1. native editable Figma text or shape;
2. fidelity-gated SVG/vector;
3. named, swappable exact raster asset or intentional raster cluster;
4. baked source pixels with recorded ownership.

An unsupported or low-confidence region must not disappear and must not be replaced by a
guessed font, fake vector, or invented layer structure. Screenshots, UI panels, receipts,
charts/graphs, tables/nutrition panels, diagrams/infographics, perspective compositions, and
inseparable product scenes default to a named source-backed raster cluster. Only independently
proven external overlays may be separated from that cluster.
